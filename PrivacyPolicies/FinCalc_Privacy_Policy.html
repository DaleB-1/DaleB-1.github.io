FinCalc Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the FinCalc app available in the Google Play Store.

Summary

1) You will be required to enter personal information such as marital status and number of children and financial information such as salary and household expenses.

2) This data is immediately wiped when you stop using the app or switch to another section.

3) The app keeps track of how many times you use it and prompts you to rate the app in the Google Play Store after every 100 uses. You can decline to rate it but I'd be very grateful if you tell me what you think about the app.
